<p><strong>The Optical Turbidimeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scattered Light Turbidimeter</li><li>Transmitted Light Turbidimeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical turbidimeter market features two main types: scattered light and transmitted light turbidimeters. Scattered light turbidimeters measure turbidity by detecting light that is scattered by particles suspended in a liquid, making them effective for high turbidity levels. In contrast, transmitted light turbidimeters quantify turbidity based on the amount of light that passes through a sample, suitable for lower turbidity levels. Both types are essential for water quality analysis across various industries, including environmental monitoring and wastewater management.</p></p>
