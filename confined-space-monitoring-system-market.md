<p><p>The Confined Space Monitoring System market is divided into two main types: Centralized Monitoring System and Distributed Monitoring System. </p><p>Centralized Monitoring Systems consolidate data from multiple confined spaces into a single control unit, allowing for real-time monitoring and quick response to hazards. In contrast, Distributed Monitoring Systems utilize multiple autonomous sensors distributed throughout various locations, enabling localized data collection and analysis. Both systems enhance safety by detecting hazardous conditions in confined spaces but cater to different operational needs and preferences.</p></p>
