<p><p>The pharmaceutical industry isolators market includes two primary types: sterility test isolators and sterility production isolators. Sterility test isolators are designed to conduct sterility testing of pharmaceutical products in a controlled environment, ensuring no contamination occurs during testing. In contrast, sterility production isolators are used for the aseptic production of pharmaceuticals, providing a sterile environment for handling and manufacturing products. Both types enhance product safety, quality, and compliance with strict regulatory standards in the pharmaceutical sector.</p></p>
