<p><strong>The Automotive Brake Caliper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Piston Caliper</li><li>Multi-Piston Caliper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive brake caliper market is primarily divided into two types: single piston calipers and multi-piston calipers. Single piston calipers feature one piston that compresses the brake pads against the rotor for efficient braking, making them suitable for lighter vehicles. In contrast, multi-piston calipers have multiple pistons, providing greater clamping force and improved performance, ideal for high-performance and heavy-duty vehicles. These variations cater to different vehicle specifications, enhancing safety and driving dynamics in the automotive industry.</p></p>
