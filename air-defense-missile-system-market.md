<p><p>The air-defense missile system market can be categorized into two main types: Man-Portable Air-Defense Systems (MANPADS) and Stationary/Platform-Mounted Air-Defense Systems. MANPADS are lightweight, portable missiles designed for easy use by infantry, offering quick response against low-flying threats. In contrast, Stationary/Platform-Mounted systems are larger, more complex installations often integrated into military vehicles or fixed sites, providing enhanced targeting capabilities and greater defensive range. Both types play crucial roles in modern military operations, addressing diverse aerial threats.</p></p>
