<p><p>The Pharmaceuticals Glass Vials market is classified into three main types based on glass composition and performance. Type I glass vials are made from borosilicate glass, offering high chemical resistance and stability for sensitive substances. Type II glass vials are soda-lime glass coated with an inner layer to enhance chemical resistance, suitable for many pharmaceuticals. Type III glass vials are regular soda-lime glass, used for non-sensitive medications. Each type caters to specific storage and compatibility needs in the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
