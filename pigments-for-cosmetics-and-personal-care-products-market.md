<p><p>The pigments market for cosmetics and personal care products is categorized into inorganic and organic types. Inorganic pigments, derived from natural minerals, offer excellent stability and UV protection, often used in foundations and powders. They provide opacity and color brightness. Organic pigments, synthesized from carbon-based compounds, deliver vibrant hues and are favored for their intense color payoff. They often require careful formulation to ensure stability and safety. Both types are essential for achieving desired aesthetics and performance in cosmetic formulations.</p></p>
