<p><strong>The 360 Degree Industrial Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spherical 360</li><li>Panoramic 360</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 360-degree industrial camera market encompasses two main types: spherical 360 and panoramic 360 cameras. Spherical 360 cameras capture images in all directions from a single point, providing immersive, true-to-life visuals suitable for applications like virtual reality. In contrast, panoramic 360 cameras focus on wide-angle views, stitching together images to create a broad perspective of the environment, which is ideal for surveillance, inspections, and monitoring in industrial settings. Both types enhance situational awareness and improve operational efficiency.</p></p>
