<p><p>Thermoplastic Polyurethane (TPU) is a versatile polymer used in various applications due to its flexibility, durability, and resistance to abrasion and chemicals. The TPU market is primarily divided into two types: aromatic TPU and aliphatic TPU. Aromatic TPU is known for its excellent mechanical properties and is generally more affordable, making it suitable for industrial applications. In contrast, aliphatic TPU offers superior UV stability and transparency, making it ideal for applications requiring aesthetics and outdoor durability, such as automotive and consumer goods.</p></p>
