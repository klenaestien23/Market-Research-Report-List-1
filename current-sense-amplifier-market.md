<p><ul><li>High-side Sensing</li><li>Low-side Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Current Sense Amplifier market is categorized into high-side and low-side sensing types. High-side sensing measures current on the positive supply line, offering better safety and protection for sensitive loads and enabling precise monitoring of power delivery. In contrast, low-side sensing measures current on the ground side, usually providing simpler designs and lower costs but may introduce ground potential issues. Both types are essential for various applications, including power management, battery monitoring, and electronic devices requiring precise current measurements.</p></p>
