<p><p>The eco-friendly solvent oil market is characterized by various types, including normal paraffin solvent oil, isoparaffin solvent oil, and cycloparaffin solvent oil. Normal paraffin solvent oil is derived from crude oil and is favored for its low toxicity and volatility. Isoparaffin solvent oil offers improved performance and is environmentally friendly due to its cleaner composition. Cycloparaffin solvent oil combines properties of both paraffins and is known for its high solvency and reduced environmental impact, making all three types essential for sustainable applications.</p></p>
