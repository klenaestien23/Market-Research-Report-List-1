<p><p>Real-Time Location Systems (RTLS) deploy various technologies to track assets and personnel. RFID uses radio waves for identification and tracking through tags. Wi-Fi leverages existing networks to determine location via signal strength. Ultrasound utilizes sound waves for precise positioning, while infrared employs light signals for close-range tracking. ZigBee offers low-power, low-data-rate wireless communication for sensor networks. Ultra-Wide Band (UWB) provides accurate, high-resolution tracking over short distances. Each type offers unique advantages suited to different industries and applications.</p></p>
