<p><p>The windscreen cleaner market is segmented into three main types: laminated glass cleaners, tempered glass cleaners, and dry-wet mixing cleaners. Laminated glass cleaners are formulated to effectively remove contaminants while protecting the glass layers. Tempered glass cleaners are designed to safeguard the strength and integrity of the glass surface during cleaning. Dry-wet mixing cleaners combine both dry and wet application methods for versatile usage, ensuring a thorough cleaning experience. Each type targets specific glass characteristics to maintain visibility and safety.</p></p>
