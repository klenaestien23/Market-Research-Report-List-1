<p><p>The Oral X-ray machines market is categorized into analog and digital X-ray types. Analog X-ray machines utilize conventional film to capture images, providing a straightforward imaging solution but with longer development times and less diagnostic flexibility. In contrast, digital X-ray machines employ electronic sensors to capture images instantly, offering superior image quality, reduced radiation exposure, and enhanced storage capabilities. The shift towards digital imaging is driven by efficiency and the growing demand for precise diagnostics in dental practices.</p></p>
