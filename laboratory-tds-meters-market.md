<p><p>Laboratory TDS meters are essential for measuring total dissolved solids in solutions, with distinctions among single channel, dual channel, and multi-channel types. Single channel meters measure TDS in one sample at a time, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Dual channel meters allow simultaneous measurement of two samples, increasing efficiency. Multi-channel meters can handle multiple samples at once, suitable for high-throughput environments. Each type caters to different laboratory needs, with varying complexity, efficiency, and budget considerations for users.</p></p>
