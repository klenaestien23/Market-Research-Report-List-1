<p><p>The compressor fluids market consists of two primary types: synthetic oil and mineral oil. Synthetic oils are chemically engineered for superior performance, offering enhanced lubrication, thermal stability, and longer service life, making them ideal for high-stress applications. Mineral oils, derived from refined petroleum products, are cost-effective and widely used in conventional compressors. While they provide adequate lubrication, they may require more frequent changes due to thermal breakdown and oxidation. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs and operational conditions.</p></p>
