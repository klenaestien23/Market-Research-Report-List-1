<p><strong>The Underwater Yacht Light Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through-hull</li><li>Surface-mount</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Underwater Yacht Light market primarily consists of two types: through-hull and surface-mount lights. Through-hull lights are installed by penetrating the hull, providing a sleek appearance and effectively illuminating the water below without obstruction. In contrast, surface-mount lights are attached externally to the hull and are easier to install, making them suitable for DIY installations. Both types enhance nighttime visibility and aesthetics, catering to different preferences and installation capabilities among yacht owners.</p></p>
