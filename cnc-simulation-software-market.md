<p><p>The CNC simulation software market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software allows users to access simulation tools and data online, offering flexibility, scalability, and easier collaboration. It typically requires a subscription model and reduces the need for extensive local IT infrastructure.
