<p><p>The aluminum oxide nanoparticles market is categorized into three primary types based on their crystal structures: α crystal, β crystal, and γ crystal. α crystal, the most stable form, is widely used in abrasives and ceramics due to its hardness. β crystal exhibits specific electrical properties, making it suitable for electronic applications. γ crystal, known for its colloidal stability, finds applications in catalysis and biomedical fields. Each type offers distinct characteristics that cater to varied industrial needs and technological advancements.</p></p>
