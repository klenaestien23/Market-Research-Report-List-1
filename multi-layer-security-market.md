<p><p>Multi-layer security involves three market types: proactive, detective, and reactive. Proactive security focuses on prevention, implementing measures to thwart potential threats before they occur, such as risk assessments and training. Detective security aims to identify and understand incidents already happening or those that have occurred, employing tools like monitoring systems and audits. Reactive security responds to incidents after they happen, emphasizing recovery and remediation through incident response plans and forensic analysis to minimize damage and restore functionality.</p></p>
