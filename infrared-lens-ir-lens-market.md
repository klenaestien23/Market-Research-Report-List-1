<p><p>The infrared lens market is primarily categorized into two types: prime infrared lenses and zoom infrared lenses. Prime infrared lenses offer fixed focal lengths, providing superior image quality and higher sensitivity to infrared light, making them ideal for specific applications such as surveillance and thermal imaging. In contrast, zoom infrared lenses provide variable focal lengths, allowing for versatile field-of-view adjustments, making them suitable for dynamic situations where distance and detail need to be quickly adjusted, enhancing flexibility in various imaging scenarios.</p></p>
