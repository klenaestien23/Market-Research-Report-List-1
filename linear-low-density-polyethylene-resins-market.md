<p><p>Linear Low Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) resins are categorized into two primary market types: Type I and Type II. Type I LLDPE is characterized by its high tensile strength and flexibility, making it ideal for film applications, like packaging and agricultural films. Type II LLDPE, on the other hand, offers enhanced puncture resistance and is recognized for its durability, often used in industrial applications, such as trash liners and heavy-duty bags. Both types serve distinct market needs based on their physical properties.</p></p>
