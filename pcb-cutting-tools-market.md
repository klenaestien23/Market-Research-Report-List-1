<p><strong>The PCB Cutting Tools Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight Type Drill</li><li>Undercut Drill</li><li>Inverse Drill</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB cutting tools market comprises several types tailored for specific applications. Straight type drills are primarily used for standard hole drilling with precision. Undercut drills feature a tapered design that allows for angled holes or recesses, ideal for creating profiles in PCB layers. Inverse drills are designed to remove material from the underside of PCBs, providing flexibility in design and component placement. Other market segments include specialized tools for unique PCB configurations and advanced drilling technologies to improve efficiency and output.</p></p>
