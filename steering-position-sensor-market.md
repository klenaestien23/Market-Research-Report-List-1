<p><strong>The Steering Position Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetic Sensor</li><li>Contacting Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steering position sensor market features two primary types: magnetic sensors and contacting sensors. Magnetic sensors utilize magnetic fields to detect the steering wheel's angle, offering high accuracy and durability with minimal wear. In contrast, contacting sensors rely on physical contact to measure the position, providing real-time feedback but potentially experiencing wear over time. Both sensor types are crucial for enhancing vehicle safety and performance by ensuring precise steering control and assisting advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS).</p></p>
