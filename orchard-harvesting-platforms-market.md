<p><p>Orchard harvesting platforms are categorized into two main types: self-propelled and towed. Self-propelled platforms are autonomous units equipped with their own engines, allowing for high mobility and versatility in varied orchard terrains. In contrast, towed platforms are designed to be connected to a tractor or utility vehicle, relying on the towing vehicle for propulsion. Both types enhance harvesting efficiency, but self-propelled units generally offer more maneuverability, while towed platforms may be more cost-effective and suitable for larger operations.</p></p>
