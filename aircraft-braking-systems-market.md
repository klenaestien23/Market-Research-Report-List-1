<p><p>The aircraft braking systems market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Aircraft Disc Brakes** are primarily used to slow down or stop the aircraft during landing and taxiing, utilizing friction created by disc and pad materials.</p><p>2. **Thrust Reversers** redirect engine thrust forward upon landing, aiding in deceleration and reducing brake wear.</p><p>3. **Air Brakes** are deployed to create additional drag, enhancing stopping power and maintaining controlled descent during landing. Each system contributes to overall safety and performance in aviation operations.</p></p>
