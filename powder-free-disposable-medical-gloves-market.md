<p><p>The powder-free disposable medical gloves market includes three primary types: PVC, nitrile, and vinyl. PVC gloves are cost-effective and provide basic protection, commonly used in low-risk situations. Nitrile gloves offer superior puncture and chemical resistance, making them ideal for high-risk environments and heavy-duty applications. Vinyl gloves are suitable for non-hazardous tasks, offering comfort and easy wear, though they provide lower protection compared to nitrile. The choice among these types depends on the specific needs of healthcare professionals and the tasks at hand.</p></p>
