<p><p>The reversed-phase HPLC (High-Performance Liquid Chromatography) columns market is primarily categorized into packed columns and capillary columns. Packed columns consist of a tightly packed stationary phase, allowing for efficient separation of a wide range of analytes in larger samples. In contrast, capillary columns, featuring narrow diameters, enable high-resolution separations with minimal sample volumes, making them ideal for sensitive applications. Both types cater to diverse analytical needs in pharmaceutical, environmental, and biochemical research, each offering unique advantages in separation efficiency and sensitivity.</p></p>
