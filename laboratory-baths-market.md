<p><p>Laboratory baths are essential equipment used for maintaining controlled temperatures in various scientific and industrial applications. The market is primarily divided into cooling baths and heated baths. Cooling baths are designed to lower temperatures, suitable for applications like preservation or chemical reactions that require a cold environment. Conversely, heated baths provide elevated temperatures for processes such as incubating samples, conducting chemical reactions, or performing sterilization. Both types cater to diverse sectors, including pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and education, ensuring precise temperature management.</p></p>
