<p><p>The Passenger Airbag (PAB) market consists of various types, primarily including frontal airbags and side airbags. Frontal airbags deploy in moderate to severe collisions, protecting the occupant's head and chest by cushioning impact. Side airbags, on the other hand, are designed to shield passengers during side-impact crashes, offering protection to the torso and head. Both types are crucial for enhancing vehicle safety, reducing injuries, and meeting regulatory standards, thereby driving demand in the automotive industry.</p></p>
